about me!

I'm Gaby, a software developer & lifelong learner currently living in Seattle with my husband and two cats.

I've had the privilege of studying Baking & Patisserie at Le Cordon Bleu Atlanta, Public Health & Nutrition at the University of Washington, and Full Stack Software Development at Ada Developers Academy.

I have 7 years of cake decorating experience, 2 years of health sciences research experience, and 9 months of programming experience.

I spend a lot of time thinking about food, cats, and how to solve real-world problems.
