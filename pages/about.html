Tinghui Jiang

Software Developer

Tinghui Jiang was born and raised in Chengdu, China. She is a first-generation college student and first-generation immigrant. She holds a Master of Public Policy degree from UCLA and a B.A double major in Political Economy and Communications from University of Washington.

Through internships, research, and volunteering, she worked with students, social workers, and food services workers in low-income communities in SoCal. Her research interests include food security, obesity prevention, and substance use, with an emphasis on social justice and community engagement.

After graduation, Tinghui relocated back to Seattle and worked as a research coordinator at UW Medicine, where she worked on projects related to early identification and treatment of sepsis, precision care in cardiac arrest, and surrogate consent for critical care patients.

She recently was accepted into Ada Developer's Academy where she is studying to become a software developer.
