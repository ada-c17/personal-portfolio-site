ABOUT ME

I'm Sophia Wagner, an entrepreneur and aspiring full-stack developer, currently studying at Ada Developers Academy. My background is in socially responsible finance. I'm a co-founder of a platform to that helps people see what banks do with their deposits. In the past I've worked on community loan access, helped ensure compliance with anti-discrimination regulations for mortgages, and researched eco-friendly banking policies.

Some things I'm interested in right now: civic technology, interesting uses of public data, efforts to increase transparency (any industry), the intersection of public policy and machine learning, and anti-corruption efforts. If you'd like to learn more about my experience or get in touch, contact me on LinkedIn.
